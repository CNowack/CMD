About this database

The Cancer Microbiome Database (CMD) integrates 16S rRNA microbiome sequencing data from 547 samples across 142 BMC cancer patients with their clinical metadata, providing a unified resource to explore microbiome composition, visualize phylogenetic relationships, and track longitudinal shifts across treatment timepoints.
